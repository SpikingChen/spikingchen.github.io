Biography

I am currently a master's student in Peking University, supervised by Prof. Xing Zhang. I received my Bachelor degree from China Agricultural University in 2021.

My major research interests lie in Multimodal Learning and Brain-inspired Intelligence, such as

  • Conditional Multi-modality Generation (Any to Any)
  • Vision-Language Pre-training (VLP)
  • Optimization and Application in Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs)

I would be very happy to receive the email if you were interested to discuss ideas or work together.
